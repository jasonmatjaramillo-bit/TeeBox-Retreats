FAQ

Common questions before a custom golf simulator project begins.

How much does a custom golf simulator cost?

Cost depends on the room size, technology package, projection approach, finish level, and whether the installation is residential or commercial.

Can you design a golf simulator for a small room?

Yes. A small-room design starts with realistic dimensions and swing clearance, then matches the right system to what the room can actually support.

Do you build commercial golf simulators?

Yes. TeeBox Retreats works on commercial golf simulators for clubs, hospitality venues, instruction environments, and entertainment-focused spaces.

Do you serve New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Pennsylvania?

Yes. The company serves NY, NJ, CT & PA with local golf simulator design and installation planning.

Can you work within my budget?

Yes. Projects can be planned in practical, premium, or phased tiers so the recommendation fits both the room and the investment range.
